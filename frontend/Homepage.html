GUIDE

Search Tasks:

On the home page, locate the search bar. Enter the keyword or phrase related to the task you want to search for. Press Enter or click on the search icon.

View Tasks:

After searching for tasks, a list of matching tasks will be displayed. Click on the task you want to view to see its details.

Delete Tasks:

To delete a task, locate the task in the list of search results. Click on the delete button or icon next to the task. Confirm the deletion if prompted.

Create Tasks:

On the home page, locate the "Create" button. Click on the "Create" button. Enter the name of the task you want to create in the text field. Press Enter or click on the "Save" button to create the task.

Start Task and Capture Screen:

After creating a task, locate the task in the list of tasks. Click on the task name to select it. Press the "Start" button to begin working on the task. As you work on the task, press the middle mouse button to capture your screen. The captured screenshots will be saved automatically.
